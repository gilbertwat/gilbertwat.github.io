    - $$d_1 = \frac{ln\frac{S_t}{K} + (r + \frac{(σ_v)^2}{2})t}{σ_s\sqrt{t}}$$

- **where:**

    - __K__=Strike price
    - __r__=Risk-free interest rate
    - __t__=Time to maturity__N__=A normal distribution​
